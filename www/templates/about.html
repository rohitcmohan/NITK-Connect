NITK Connect

NITK Connect is an official platform by the NITK Student Council where all students of NITK can get access to information related to college life. Every student club or community is represented on the app and can share news related to current activities. This app also has live updates which keep you posted about every major event in our college. It aims to bridge the gap between all NITKians, and allows us to share ideas in our college. NITK Connect will continue to improve with more features and become more engaging with every update.
